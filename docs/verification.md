# Verification

## Purpose

Verification is how the event confirms that participants met the published requirements.

Verification is based on public evidence and final presentations.

## Builder Verification

To verify Builder qualification, participants must be able to show:

- the official public repository
- visible commit history
- all required weekly update links
- a live demo
- a deployed public product link

## {{sponsored_track_name}} Verification

To verify {{sponsored_track_name}} qualification, participants must be able to show:

- proof that the project met every Builder Pie requirement
- proof of payment gating
- a live demo of real functionality
- the official public repository
- all required weekly update links
- a deployed public product link
- any ecosystem-specific evidence required by {{sponsored_track_name}} rules

## Real User Verification

To verify Real User qualification, participants must be able to show:

- proof that the project qualified as a Builder
- proof of at least one paying user
- the customer acquisition story
- public evidence of the above

## Feedback Verification

To verify Feedback qualification, participants must be able to show:

- the original feedback request in the official feedback forum
- the volunteer response or coordination in that thread
- evidence of live feedback sessions
- recordings for those sessions
- enough information to validate credits earned

The Discord thread is the coordination path. Qualification still depends on the completed recorded session and the supporting evidence.

For clarity:

- Feedback Pie credits are earned by the feedback giver, not by the builder receiving feedback.
- A builder may choose whether to accept a volunteer before the session starts.

## Verification Standard

The standard is verifiable public participation.

If required evidence is missing, incomplete, or not public where required, qualification may be denied.

## Final Presentation Checklist

Builders should present:

- live demo
- repo link
- all weekly update links
- deployed product link
- any track-specific evidence (e.g., ecosystem mainnet evidence for {{sponsored_track_name}})
