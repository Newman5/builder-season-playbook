# Tweet update template
Posts on X must follow a certain format.  The intent is to capture progress on the project and "build in public".  It also is a way to form a cohort of participants. There are optional elements, but the required elements are the # tags.

At least one qualifying public tweet is required every week.

Optional elements:
- week number
- links to the repo or product

Each required weekly tweet must:

- clearly show project progress
- include `#gimbalabs`
- include `#pieceofpie`
- include `#hackathon`
- mention `@gimbalabs`

Missing a required week results in disqualification.
